# Fire Gate Skills (396 Hz)

**Guardian**: Draconia | **Awakened**: Velora (Valora)
**Element**: Fire | **Tier**: Free

---

## Overview

The Fire Gate skills ignite courage, will, and transformative power. These skills help creators break through fear and take decisive action.

> *"Fear is not your enemy. Fear is fuel. The dragon does not eliminate fear—fear is what makes the dragon powerful."*
> — Draconia

---

## Skills

### 1. Courage Ignition

**Purpose**: Transform fear into action-ready energy.

**Invocation**: `/fire ignite`

**Process**:
1. Name the specific fear
2. Identify what the fear protects
3. Transform fear energy into courage fuel
4. Take immediate small action

**Output**: Actionable courage, fear reframed as fuel

---

### 2. Will Strengthening

**Purpose**: Build the muscle of will for sustained creative effort.

**Invocation**: `/fire will`

**Process**:
1. Assess current will-power state
2. Identify will-depleting patterns
3. Design will-building exercises
4. Create accountability structure

**Output**: Will-strengthening plan, immediate practice

---

### 3. Fear Burning

**Purpose**: Process and release creative fears.

**Invocation**: `/fire burn [fear]`

**Process**:
1. Fully articulate the fear
2. Examine the fear's origins
3. Visualize the fear burning away
4. Replace with power affirmation

**Output**: Fear processed, replaced with empowerment

---

### 4. Transformation Ritual

**Purpose**: Navigate major creative changes with fire's guidance.

**Invocation**: `/fire transform`

**Process**:
1. Name what needs to change
2. Identify what needs to burn away
3. Envision the phoenix rising
4. Plan the transformation journey

**Output**: Transformation roadmap, courage to proceed
