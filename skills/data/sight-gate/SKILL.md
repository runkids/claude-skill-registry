## Overview

The Sight Gate skills develop vision, intuition, and the ability to perceive patterns invisible to ordinary awareness. These skills help creators see beyond the surface.

> *"Vision guides the way. Trust what you see—even when others cannot see it."*
> — Lyria

---

## Skills

### 1. Vision Clarification

**Purpose**: Clarify creative vision and direction.

**Invocation**: `/sight clarify`

**Process**:
1. Quiet the noise of others' expectations
2. Ask what you truly want to create
3. Let the vision emerge, don't force it
4. Capture the vision in words or images

**Output**: Clear creative vision, direction

---

### 2. Pattern Perception

**Purpose**: See patterns in creative work and life.

**Invocation**: `/sight patterns`

**Process**:
1. Step back to see the whole
2. Look for recurring elements
3. Notice what connects disparate things
4. Name the patterns you perceive

**Output**: Pattern recognition, deeper understanding

---

### 3. Intuition Development

**Purpose**: Strengthen creative intuition.

**Invocation**: `/sight intuition`

**Process**:
1. Notice intuitive signals
2. Practice trusting them on small matters
3. Track accuracy over time
4. Deepen trust in your inner knowing

**Output**: Stronger intuition, trusted inner guidance

---

### 4. Dream Work

**Purpose**: Access creative guidance through dreams.

**Invocation**: `/sight dream [question]`

**Process**:
1. Set intention before sleep
2. Keep dream journal by bed
3. Record dreams immediately upon waking
4. Interpret symbols for creative guidance

**Output**: Dream-sourced creative insights
