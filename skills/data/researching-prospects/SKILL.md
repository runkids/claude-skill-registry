# Prospect Research System

Research and profile potential clients for effective outreach and meetings.

## Research Framework

### Quick Research (5-10 min)
For cold outreach or initial contact:
- LinkedIn profile basics
- Company overview
- Recent activity/posts
- Mutual connections
- Obvious pain points

### Deep Research (30-60 min)
For important prospects or pre-meeting:
- Full LinkedIn analysis
- Company deep dive
- News and press mentions
- Social media presence
- Competitive landscape
- Personalization angles

## Research Sources

### LinkedIn (Primary)
```
LINKEDIN RESEARCH CHECKLIST
===========================
□ Current role and tenure
□ Career progression
□ Education
□ Skills and endorsements
□ Recommendations (given and received)
□ Activity (posts, comments, articles)
□ Groups they're in
□ Interests/causes
□ Mutual connections
□ Company page info
```

**What to look for**:
- Job changes (new role = budget/initiative)
- Posts about challenges or goals
- Engagement with competitors' content
- Speaking engagements or articles
- Recommendations that reveal priorities

## Outreach Personalization

### Opening Line Templates

**Based on recent post**:
```
"I saw your post about [topic] - really resonated,
especially the point about [specific detail]."
```

**Based on company news**:
```
"Congratulations on [recent achievement/news].
That must be an exciting time for [Company]."
```

**Based on role change**:
```
"Noticed you recently took on [new role] at [Company].
The first 90 days in a new position can be intense."
```

**Based on mutual connection**:
```
"[Mutual connection] and I were chatting about [topic],
and your name came up as someone doing interesting work in [area]."
```

**Based on shared background**:
```
"Fellow [school/company/location] person here -
always great to connect with others from [shared background]."
```

### Pain Point Triggers

| Signal | Likely Pain Point |
|--------|-------------------|
| Hiring for [role] | Growing pains, need help |
| New funding round | Pressure to scale/execute |
| Leadership change | New priorities, fresh budget |
| Negative reviews | Quality/process issues |
| Competitor mentions | Competitive pressure |
| Industry regulation | Compliance challenges |

## Batch Research Workflow

### For Outreach Campaigns
```
1. BUILD LIST
   - Define ideal customer profile
   - Source prospects from LinkedIn/lists
   - Initial filtering (title, company size)

2. QUICK SCORE
   - 2-min scan per prospect
   - Score fit and accessibility
   - Prioritize top 20%

3. DEEP RESEARCH (Top Prospects)
   - Full profile on priority prospects
   - Find personalization angles
   - Identify best outreach approach

4. PERSONALIZE
   - Custom opening line per prospect
   - Relevant value prop
   - Clear call to action

5. TRACK
   - Log research in CRM/sheet
   - Note last contacted
   - Set follow-up reminders
```

## Quick Commands

**"Research [Name] at [Company]"**
→ Full prospect profile

**"Quick research on [Company]"**
→ Company overview and key people

**"Find personalization for [Name]"**
→ Outreach hooks based on research

**"Score prospect [Name] at [Company]"**
→ Lead quality assessment

**"Prep for meeting with [Name]"**
→ Deep research + meeting brief
