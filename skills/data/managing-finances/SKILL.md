## Tax Obligations

### Federal Taxes (Single-Member LLC)
- LLC is "pass-through" entity
- Report on personal tax return (Schedule C)
- Self-employment tax: 15.3% on net profit
- Income tax: Based on total income and bracket
- **Estimated taxes due**: April 15, June 15, Sept 15, Jan 15

### Massachusetts Taxes
- No separate LLC tax for single-member
- Report on personal MA return
- MA income tax rate: 5% flat
