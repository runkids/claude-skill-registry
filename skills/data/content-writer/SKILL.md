# Alpha AI Content Writer

## Voice & Tone

- **Direct and motivational** - Like a mentor talking to peers
- **Practical, not theoretical** - Focus on action and results
- **Inclusive** - Speak to non-technical professionals ages 30-60+
- **Fraternal** - Brothers helping brothers

## Key Phrases

- "Bros" / "Brothers"
- "On the Shield"
- "Built for action, not theory"
- "If you can text, you can use AI"
- "This is what separates talkers from doers"

## Writing Examples

### Module Description
Bad: "This video covers AI productivity techniques and workflow optimization strategies."

Good: "Practical tips for using AI to boost your daily productivity. Research, summarization, document creation, and problem-solving - all in one video."

### Motivational Text
Bad: "Complete the exercises to learn AI skills."

Good: "Here's what separates the guys who *talk* about AI from the guys who actually **use** it."

### Footer Style
- "First of All, Servants of All, We Shall Transcend All"
- "Alpha Phi Alpha Fraternity, Inc. - Beta Chapter"

## Content Structure

1. **Lead with the benefit** - What will they be able to do?
2. **Keep it short** - Busy professionals, respect their time
3. **End with action** - What should they do next?
