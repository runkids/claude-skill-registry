# Meeting Preparation System

Prepare for and follow up on client meetings effectively.

## Meeting Types

| Type | Duration | Prep Time | Key Focus |
|------|----------|-----------|-----------|
| Discovery Call | 30 min | 15 min | Learn needs, qualify |
| Pitch/Proposal | 45-60 min | 1-2 hrs | Present solution, close |
| Kickoff | 30-60 min | 30 min | Align on scope, next steps |
| Working Session | 1-2 hrs | 15 min | Execute deliverables |
| Check-in | 15-30 min | 10 min | Progress, blockers |
| Follow-up | 30 min | 15 min | Review, questions |

## Pre-Meeting Checklist

### 24 Hours Before
```
□ Confirm meeting time and attendees
□ Review previous interactions/notes
□ Research attendees (LinkedIn)
□ Prepare agenda
□ Gather relevant materials
□ Test tech (if video call)
□ Prepare questions to ask
□ Set meeting goals
```

### 1 Hour Before
```
□ Review agenda and talking points
□ Open relevant documents/demos
□ Clear notifications
□ Have water nearby
□ Quiet environment ready
□ Backup contact method noted
```

## Discovery Call Template

### Agenda (30 min)
```
DISCOVERY CALL AGENDA
=====================
Client: [Name]
Company: [Company]
Date: [Date]
Duration: 30 minutes

1. INTRODUCTIONS (3 min)
   - Brief background on Support Forge
   - Their role and background

2. UNDERSTAND THEIR SITUATION (10 min)
   - What prompted reaching out?
   - Current challenges/pain points
   - What have they tried?

3. GOALS & TIMELINE (7 min)
   - What does success look like?
   - Timeline/urgency
   - Decision process

4. EXPLORE FIT (5 min)
   - How we might help
   - Relevant experience
   - Initial thoughts on approach

5. NEXT STEPS (5 min)
   - Proposal/follow-up timing
   - Additional info needed
   - Schedule next meeting
```

### Discovery Questions
```
SITUATION
- Tell me about your current setup/process
- What tools are you using today?
- How long have you been dealing with this?

PROBLEM
- What's the biggest challenge you're facing?
- What happens if this doesn't get solved?
- How is this impacting your business/team?

IMPLICATION
- What have you tried so far?
- Why hasn't that worked?
- What would solving this mean for you?

NEED/PAYOFF
- What does the ideal solution look like?
- What's your timeline for this?
- What's the budget range you're working with?

DECISION
- Who else is involved in this decision?
- What's your process for moving forward?
- What would make this a "yes" for you?
```

## Pitch Meeting Template

### Agenda (45-60 min)
```
PITCH MEETING AGENDA
====================
Client: [Name]
Company: [Company]
Date: [Date]
Duration: 45-60 minutes

1. RAPPORT & CONTEXT (5 min)
   - Reconnect from discovery
   - Confirm understanding of needs

2. PRESENT SOLUTION (15 min)
   - Approach overview
   - Specific deliverables
   - Timeline
   - Differentiation

3. DEMONSTRATE VALUE (10 min)
   - Relevant case study/example
   - Demo if applicable
   - Results they can expect

4. INVESTMENT & TERMS (10 min)
   - Pricing options
   - What's included
   - Payment terms

5. ADDRESS CONCERNS (10 min)
   - Questions and objections
   - Clarifications

6. CLOSE & NEXT STEPS (5 min)
   - Gauge interest
   - Propose next step
   - Timeline to decision
```

### Pitch Talking Points
```
OPENING
- Thank them for time
- Reference previous conversation
- Confirm agenda

VALUE PROPOSITION
- "Based on what you shared, here's what I recommend..."
- Focus on outcomes, not features
- Connect to their specific pain points

DIFFERENTIATION
- Why Support Forge vs alternatives
- Relevant experience
- Personal attention advantage

PRICING TRANSITION
- "Let me walk you through the investment..."
- Present with confidence
- Tie to value delivered

HANDLING OBJECTIONS
- Listen fully before responding
- Acknowledge the concern
- Provide reassurance/evidence
- Check if addressed

CLOSING
- "Based on our conversation, I think [package] is the right fit"
- "What questions do you have before we move forward?"
- Propose specific next step
```
