# Explainable Reasoning

Use this skill to generate clear reasoning trails.

## Workflow

1) State inputs and constraints.
2) Show decision steps and tradeoffs.
3) Highlight uncertainties and risks.
4) Provide final decision with rationale.

## Output expectations

- Provide stepwise rationale and uncertainty notes.
